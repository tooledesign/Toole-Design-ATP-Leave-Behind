Our Practice

Expert Statewide ATP Leadership

Toole Design has developed more than 350 bicycle and pedestrian plans for communities across North America — from statewide policy frameworks to regional strategies and local networks. Our active transportation planning team has unparalleled experience in the field of statewide active transportation planning, encompassing a range of statewide planning and policy efforts throughout the United States.

Statewide active transportation plans (ATPs) are among the most complex and consequential planning efforts we undertake. They span vast geographies, serve diverse communities, and must navigate the competing priorities of state agencies, regional partners, and local stakeholders. No two statewide ATPs are alike — and that’s exactly why Toole Design’s customized, curiosity-driven approach sets our plans apart.

Our statewide ATP practice is supported by a national team of planners, engineers, and data scientists who combine expertise in GIS analysis, community engagement, design guidance, and implementation strategy to create plans that make a difference.
